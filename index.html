About Me

My name is Chelsey McKinney, and I am a Southerner living in the Twin Cities! I'm originally from Memphis, TN, but I moved to the "mini-apple" for a job working with children. I have enjoyed my time in Minneapolis, and I am enjoying things more since I have started the coding camp!

I have two brothers: a twin brother, and an older brother. My birthday is in January, so my favorite holiday is New Year's Eve. I love how so many people style their parties with white lights. White lights to me, are the most beautiful.
